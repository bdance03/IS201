Experience

Quality is everything

Dance Brothers Painting has been in business for 29 years. My father started the company to pay for college and has handed it down to each of his six brothers until my uncle handed it off to me. I have 5+ years of professional experience painting and am highly trained to do an amazing job.

We specialize in the exterior of the home. We do everything from the body color, the trim, the garages, gutters, doors..etc

We professionally prepare your home for the paint. We will pressure wash, scrape, sand, and prime where needed, and paper and tape any windows or other items that won't get painted.

What we do

  • Pressure wash the home
  • Scrape and sand old paint that is peeling
  • Prime dry and bare wood
  • Mask off windows, doors, garage, lights..etc
  • Spray the body of the home
  • Trim the home
  • Spray any doors and garages
  • Walk around and touch up
  • Walk around with you before finishing
